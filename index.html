Posts per Political Leaning

This section shows the posts per political leaning per topic.

By using the drop down menu at the top of the section, you can select the different topics to explore. The below three section will then display the posts that were published by groups/pages from a conservative, neutral or liberal leaning. If you click on any section, you should see a scroll at the right that will allow you to explore more posts about any political leaning related to the selected topic.

You can also translate the posts to any language using the drop down menu right above this button.
